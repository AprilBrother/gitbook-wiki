## Description

Micro SD Shield. It perfect match with ESPea and ESPea32.

The ESPea Micro SD Shield uses:

`D5, D6, D7, D8, 3V3 and GND`

The shield uses SPI bus pins:

  - D5 = CLK
  - D6 = MISO
  - D7 = MOSI
  - D8 = CS
